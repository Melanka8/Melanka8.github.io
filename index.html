SOUP RECIPES

Creamy Chicken Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 1 onion, chopped

Instructions:

  • In a large pot, combine chicken breast, mixed vegetables, chopped onion, and enough water to cover.
  • Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender.
  • Remove chicken from pot, shred with two forks, and return to pot.
  • Stir in cream and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.

EGGS

Shakshuka for One

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin

Instructions:

  • Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onion, bell pepper, and minced garlic. Cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  • Add canned tomatoes and cumin. Simmer until sauce thickens slightly, about 10 minutes.
  • Make two wells in the sauce and crack eggs into them. Cover and cook until eggs are set to your liking, about 5 minutes.
  • Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot with crusty bread.

CHICKEN

Champagne Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup champagne

Instructions:

  • In a shallow dish, combine flour, salt, and pepper. Dredge chicken breasts in flour mixture, shaking off excess.
  • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken breasts and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 5 minutes per side.
  • Remove chicken from skillet and keep warm. Add champagne to skillet, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan.
  • Return chicken to skillet and simmer until chicken is cooked through and sauce is slightly reduced, about 10 minutes.
  • Serve chicken hot, topped with sauce and garnished with chopped parsley.
